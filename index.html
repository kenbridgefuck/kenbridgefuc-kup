About KenBridge School

KenBridge School imparts education using technology where children are encouraged to have a practical and joyful educational journey. This method of teaching, along with an integrated curriculum encourages students to become life-long learners. Learning beyond the realm of books also gives the student a head start.

Facilities

KenBridge School focuses on academics with appropriate co-curricular experiences,
which develops Academically brilliant students well equipped with life skills to navigate the world successfully.

Academic Approach

Develops Critical Thinking, Creative Thinking, Collaborative Learning, Communication Skills Comprehensive Growth and Community Consciousness.

Academic Facilities

E-learning in every Smart Classroom with a world class software from Next Education’s TeachNext Digital classroom - The best digital content for E-learning.

read more

Building and Campus

5 Acre Lush Green Campus with specious and well-ventilated classrooms. Separate floors for Kindergarten, Primary and Senior Sections. Well-equipped Labs.

Sports Facilities

200 meter Athletic Track for Athletics and general fitness. Sports ground for field games like Football, Cricket, Tennis andHockey. Free training facility by trained coaches.

Extra Curriculur Activities

Well- Planned Education Program with emphasis on Moral and Ethical values. Guest Lectures and Special Workshops for Personality Development by Experts.
